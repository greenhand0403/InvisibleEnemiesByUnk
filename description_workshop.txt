Echoed World - Invisible Traps & Enemies
回声世界 - 隐形陷阱与敌怪

==================================================

English
--------

This is a small gameplay challenge mod.

All traps and hostile enemies are rendered as if they were painted with Echo Paint.
They become invisible unless the player equips Spectre Goggles.

✔ Traps are invisible
✔ Hostile enemies are invisible
✔ Hostile projectiles remain invisible

The effect is purely visual and client-side.
Enemies still behave normally — you just can’t see them without proper equipment.

This mod is designed for:
- Hardcore challenge runs
- Hardcore multiplayer chaos
- "Trust nothing" dungeon exploration
- Psychological warfare with friends

Config Options:
- Toggle invisible traps
- Toggle invisible enemies

Compatible with most content mods.
Does not modify AI or stats — only rendering.

--------------------------------------------------

中文
--------

这是一个小型玩法挑战模组。

所有陷阱与敌对敌怪都会被视为涂上“回声漆”，
除非玩家佩戴【幽灵护目镜】，否则无法看到它们。

✔ 陷阱隐形
✔ 敌怪隐形
✔ 敌方弹幕隐形

该效果仅影响视觉表现，不会修改敌怪AI或数值。

适合：
- 高难度挑战玩法
- 多人联机整活
- 地牢探险极限模式
- 和朋友心理战

配置项：
- 开关陷阱隐形
- 开关敌怪隐形

兼容大多数内容Mod。
仅修改渲染逻辑，不改动游戏机制。
